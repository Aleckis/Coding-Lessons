Dr. Norman Borlaug was an American agronomist who led initiatives worldwide that contributed to the extensive increases in agricultural production termed the Green Revolution. He was awarded the Nobel Peace Prize in 1970 for his contributions to world peace through increasing food supply. He is considered by many as the father of the Green Revolution and has been called "the man who saved more lives than anyone who has ever lived" .
